About me

I’m an undergraduate student at Tufts University in Medford, MA, majoring in Computer Engineering and minoring in Computer Science. I’m particularly interested in hardware and software implementation in robotics, as well as web application programming and circuit system design. In the past, I mainly work with C++, ROS, microcontrollers, and sensors. Lately, I also use basic front-end tools (HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap) and back-end framework (Node.js) for my personal projects.

Outside of school, I'm a big sports fan. I love watching Army football and Celtics basketball in my free time. On campus, I'm an avid member of the Vietnamese Student Club (VSC), the Tufts Club Basketball team, IEEE, and the Tufts Computer Science Exchange (CSX). I also co-host a hip-hop themed radio show every Sunday in the Medford/Somerville area.
